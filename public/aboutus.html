About Us

Human beings have migrated from one place to another in search of better opportunities since time immemorial. From being 'hunter gatherers' to 'life travelers', our goals for exploring new cities may have changed, but the challenges that one faces still prevail.

Yes, we no longer need to worry about wild animals, but the task of building yourself a 'home away from home' can be exhausting. From arranging for basic requirements of one's quotidian existence like food, laundry, etc., to dealing with loneliness, social anxiety, and boredom, the entire process results in copious amounts of stress in one's life.

According to WIRED, the pandemic has turned even most of the extroverts into introverts, and hence, it's been hard to re-enter the 'offline mode' of life. In a situation like that, Connectify is here to assist you!
